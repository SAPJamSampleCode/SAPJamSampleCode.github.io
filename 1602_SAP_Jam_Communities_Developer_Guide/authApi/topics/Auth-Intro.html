SAP Jam Authentication and Authorization

Client applications using SAP Jam OData or REST APIs have two options for providing authorization and authentication for their users: an OAuth1.0a 3-Legged workflow, or SAML assertions from a trusted identity provider. The OAuth1.0a workflow is best for client applications without access to a SAML identity provider (IDP), although it requires some interaction with the end user. The SAML assertions from a trusted IDP configured in the SAP Jam Admin console by your company administrator. Additionally, there is the possibility of single-use tokens, although this approach is better suited to granting immediate, short-term access to single pages.
